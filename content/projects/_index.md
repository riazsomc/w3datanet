### Self-Hosted AI & Knowledge Systems Architecture

Design and deployment of a production-grade, fully self-hosted AI assistant within a secure national infrastructure.

The system leverages Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) to deliver precise, knowledge-base-grounded guidance across digital health platforms, identity systems, and health information ecosystems.

Architectural emphasis included secure inference pipelines, high-concurrency model serving, controlled knowledge retrieval, and strict institutional response constraints.

---

### Digital Health Platforms & Interoperability

Design and operationalization of interoperability-aware digital health systems, including large-scale deployments of national health information platforms.

Work involved metadata harmonization, standards alignment, system integration strategies, and the development of middleware architectures enabling structured data exchange.

---

### Identity & Access Architecture

Deployment and integration of federated authentication frameworks supporting secure access across distributed digital platforms.

Focus areas included trust models, identity governance, and secure gateway design within institutional environments.

---

### Email Infrastructure & Security Systems

Design and deployment of enterprise-grade email infrastructure and security mechanisms, including domain authentication, reporting analytics, and operational monitoring systems.

Includes development of specialized tools such as DMARC analysis dashboards and administrative plugins.

---

### Health Data & Terminology Systems

Technical contributions to classification systems, metadata dictionaries, terminology governance, and standards-driven data structures supporting national health information ecosystems.

---

### Infrastructure & Virtualization Engineering

Design and management of virtualization environments, infrastructure resilience strategies, and system performance optimization across distributed services.
