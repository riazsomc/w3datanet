This article explains Bangladesh’s **Amar Swasthyo (আমার স্বাস্থ্য)** platform, a national initiative that enables citizens to obtain a **unique health ID (e-health ID)** and access their personal health records through a unified digital system.

---

## What is Amar Swasthyo?

**আমার স্বাস্থ্য (Amar Swasthyo)** is a national digital health platform developed under the Ministry of Health and Family Welfare.

It allows every citizen in Bangladesh to create a **personal health profile** linked to a **unique health ID**, often referred to as:

- e-health ID  
- unique health ID  
- e-health card  
- health card in Bangladesh  

Through this system, individuals can securely access and manage their health-related information in one place.

---

## How the Unique Health ID Works

The system generates a **unique e-health ID** for each individual through identity verification:

- **NID (National ID)** for adults  
- **Birth Registration Number (BRN)** for individuals under 18  

Once verified, a citizen receives a persistent digital identity within the national health system.

This unique health ID in Bangladesh acts as the foundation for linking:

- clinical records  
- laboratory reports  
- vaccination history  

into a single, unified profile.

---

## What Information Is Available in the Health Profile?

The Amar Swasthyo platform is designed to consolidate multiple types of health data into one accessible interface.

A citizen’s personal health profile may include:

- Clinical visit records  
- Laboratory test results  
- Vaccination history  
- Other health-related data generated through healthcare services  

This transforms fragmented records into a **continuous digital health history**.

---

## Why Amar Swasthyo Matters

The introduction of a **unique health ID and e-health card in Bangladesh** represents a major shift in how health data is managed.

Instead of disconnected systems, Amar Swasthyo creates:

- A **single source of truth** for individual health data  
- A **portable health record** accessible across facilities  
- A foundation for **[interoperable health information systems](/work/)**  

This is particularly important in a system where patients often receive care from multiple providers.

---

## A Step Toward Integrated Digital Health Infrastructure

Amar Swasthyo is not just a citizen-facing application. It is a foundational component of Bangladesh’s broader digital health architecture.

By introducing a **unique health ID in Bangladesh**, the system enables:

- Linking patient data across multiple health systems  
- Supporting continuity of care  
- Improving data availability for service delivery  

It also creates the basis for integrating future health services into a unified ecosystem.

## Accessing the Platform

Citizens can access the Amar Swasthyo platform through:

👉 Official platform: https://amarswasthyo.mohfw.gov.bd/

From there, users can:

- Register using NID or Birth Registration Number  
- Obtain their e-health ID  
- Access their personal health records

### How to get a unique health ID in Bangladesh?
Citizens can register through the Amar Swasthyo platform using NID or Birth Registration Number.

### What is an e-health card in Bangladesh?
It refers to the digital health identity that allows access to personal health records.

### Can children get a health ID?
Yes, individuals under 18 can register using their Birth Registration Number (BRN).
